Vision


We envision countries internationally, where all individuals in need, regardless of their background, are able to have equal access to quality healthcare. We seek to address the geographical barriers that stop rural and remote communities from getting the healthcare they need.

Mission


Our mission is to improve healthcare access and outcomes for underdeveloped countries by providing affordable high-quality telemedicine services. Our aim is to bridge the gap between patients and healthcare access, within these countries.

Background


Established in March of 2023, the poor health services due to inadequate health workers and lack of medical supplies in Kwale County inspired us to begin our journey in paving the way for a healthier future.


How It Started

Virtual Medical Missions was a dream birthed out of the desire to provide long term medical access to areas of the world that do not have proper health care. The CEO of VMM presented this vision to the Innovators Club at Eastvale STEM Academy in March of 2023, and they soon partnered with this dream. Teams of computer science students, engineering students, and pre-medical students joined together to build an online platform that would allow VMM to provide E-visits and telemedicine encounters to underserved patients worldwide. These brilliant minds have collaborated with the physicians and board members of VMM to create our web portal that serves patients internationally.
