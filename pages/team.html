Board of Directors

Tiffany Peraino, the founder of Virtual Medical Missions, has had a heart for medical missions since she was a young child. In college, she started doing medical missions in Dominican Republic every summer. During the pandemic, her practice adjusted to meet patients needs by opening a telemedicine clinic. She realized that telemedicine has the potential to bring medical care to anyone, anywhere. Her passion for medical missions awakened a desire to use telemedicine as a resource to bring medical care to areas that have minimal access to healthcare. Driven by the love of Jesus, she hopes to change lives internationally by equipping people with the tools they need to live a healthy life.

Ms. Tiffany Peraino (PA-C)

CEO of Virtual Medical Missions

Dave Underwood has been the CEO of Island Encounter Missions since 2001. He has been engaged in humanitarian aide domestic and abroad for decades and has experience partnering with like-minded organizations. He sits as director / advisor to 15 various non-profit organizations. He has a Doctorate of Letters in Finance and a Master's of Business Administration from Pepperdine University. He owns and operates his own accounting firm in San Diego since 1985.

Dr. Dave Underwood (PhD)

Treasurer of Virtual Medical Missions

Lee Ewin, MD is a Board Certified Family Medicine physician who founded the Kaiser Permanente Moreno Valley clinic in 1987 and developed several electronic health record (EHR) systems and continued as the lead ambulatory EHR physician for the next 17 years. He helped in the EHR, including the establishment of electronic medical coding support, electronic regulatory compliance tools for physicians, and in 2004 co-founded and was the CEO of Geondan Health Solutions. Dr. Ewin retired from the SCPMG partnership in 2020, but still works part time seeing patients and leading both artificial intelligence and genetic testing projects for Kaiser Permanente.

Dr. Lee Ewin (MD)

Cheif Technical Officer

After finishing her time in academic medicine Dr. Aimee French decided to open her own practice in Corona and Riverside and give back to the community. Dr. French is a Volunteer Clinical Faculty member for Pulmonary/Critical Care Fellowship at UC, Irvine, is the past Chairman of the Leadership Board for the American Lung Association – San Bernardino and Riverside Counties and has spent countless hours speaking at patient events in both Riverside and Corona areas. In 2016 and 2017, Dr. French was awarded the Best 40 Under 40 by the Corona Chamber of Commerce, she was also recently selected as one of the Top Doctors in the Corona area by West Coast Magazine.

Dr. Aimee French (MD)
